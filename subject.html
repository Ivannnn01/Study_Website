📋 File Format Guide
Column A
Chapter Number
A whole number, e.g. 1, 2, 10
→
Column B
Chapter Name
Any text, e.g. Introduction to Mechanics
✓ Headers are optional — the importer auto-detects and skips any row where Column A is not a number. You can include or omit a header row.
✓ Both .csv and .xlsx / .xls (Excel) files are accepted.
✓ Chapters will be sorted by number after import, regardless of order in the file.
! Excel support requires an internet connection (loads the SheetJS library). CSV works fully offline.
Example CSV (no header):
1,Introduction to Kinematics
2,Newton's Laws of Motion
3,Work, Energy and Power
10,Electric Fields
Example CSV (with header — also fine):
Chapter Number,Chapter Name
1,Introduction to Kinematics
2,Newton's Laws of Motion
